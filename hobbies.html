Nick Qualls & His Hobbies

Nick Qualls is a man of many talents and hobbies, is an inspiration to all who know him. Though he is known for his impeccable work ethic and dedication to his profession, Nick also knows how to have fun when he is not busy working. He is a true lover of the outdoors and enjoys playing golf, football, and baseball. He is also an avid collector of cards and an accomplished fisherman.One of Nick's most cherished possessions is his Mickey Mantle Rookie card, which he keeps safe and close to his heart. He is a devoted fan of the Indianapolis Colts, Oklahoma Sooners, and St. Louis Cardinals, and he loves nothing more than spending time outdoors, whether it's playing ball or fishing. However, in recent years, Nick's hobbies have shifted somewhat, as he is now a proud father of two beautiful twin baby girls. Spending time with his daughters has become his greatest pleasure and pastime. He loves nothing more than being a doting dad, and his girls are his pride and joy. Another one of Nick's passions is his love for Bourbon. He has an extensive collection of over two hundred and fifty bottles of bourbon and is considered a connoisseur of the finest bourbons in the world. Nick even stars as the @travelingbourbonman on TikTok, where he shares his love for bourbon with over 3500 followers.Despite his many interests and accomplishments, Nick is known for being incredibly laid-back and easy-going. He is someone who enjoys good conversation and can spend hours talking about anything, from sports to fishing to bourbon, and everything in between. He is a man who lives life to the fullest and finds joy in every moment.
